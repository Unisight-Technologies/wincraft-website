SC 140 TT

This series for sliding doors is suitable for the realization of lift and slide solutions. The series was designed for the construction of high quality frames. The robustness of the chassis allows for the construction of large windows. A typical example maybe a glass patio, reaching up to the ceiling.

It is designed to facilitate the handling of heavy frames up till 400kg, makes the system very practical and functional. Infact, heavy glass can be installed without having its weight affect the maneuverability; it remains fluid and light.
